# Privacy Policy for TailorMyResume

*Last Updated: December 10, 2025*

TailorMyResume ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome Extension collects, uses, and discloses your information.

## 1. Information We Collect
We collect the following information only when you explicitly use the extension's features:
*   **Resume Data:** The text content of the resume files (PDF or DOCX) that you upload to the extension.
*   **Job Description Data:** The text content of the job posting page you are viewing when you click "Tailor Resume".
*   **User Settings:** Your name and contact information if you choose to save them in the extension settings.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use your information solely for the purpose of providing the resume tailoring service:
*   To analyze the match between your resume and a specific job description.
*   To generate a rewritten, tailored version of your resume.

## 3. Data Sharing and Third Parties
We do not sell your data. However, to provide our AI-powered features, we must share specific data with our AI provider:
*   **xAI (Grok):** When you click "Tailor Resume", your Resume Data and the Job Description Data are sent to xAI's API (Grok) for processing.
    Please review [xAI's Privacy Policy](https://x.ai/privacy) to understand how they handle data.

## 4. Data Storage
*   **Local Storage:** Your resume text and settings are stored locally on your device using Chrome's Local Storage API. We do not maintain a database of your personal information.
*   **Transient Processing:** Data sent to our backend server is processed in memory and is not permanently stored in a database. It is passed directly to the AI provider and the result is returned to you.

## 5. Your Consent
By using TailorMyResume, you consent to the processing of your data as described in this policy.
